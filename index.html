How to Play

Estimate the centroid* of the blue squares.

  1. TAP a cell to place your guess
  2. VALIDATE via the button or double-tapping

Each round starts with 10 points.
Lose 1 per second after 3s.
Lose 1 per cell off-target.
Perfect Score is 100.

*A centroid is the geometric center, or "average position" of all points, within a two-dimensional shape or three-dimensional solid. Often referred to as the center of mass (for uniform density), it represents the point where lines of symmetry intersect and acts as the center of gravity.
